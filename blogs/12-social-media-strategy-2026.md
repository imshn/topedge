# Social Media Marketing Strategy: Step-by-Step Plan for 2026

**Published:** July 2026 | **Reading Time:** 9 min | **By:** Top Edge MediaWorks

## Social Media Strategy: 90-Day Roadmap

Most businesses post randomly and hope. Here's a systematic approach.

---

## Phase 1: Audit (Week 1)

- Which platforms reach your audience? (LinkedIn for B2B, Instagram for B2C, TikTok for Gen-Z)
- Who are your competitors on social?
- What content gets engagement?
- Current follower growth rate?

---

## Phase 2: Content Pillars (Week 2)

Define 3-4 content themes:
1. Educational (teach your audience)
2. Entertaining (keep them engaged)
3. Promotional (sell your products)
4. Community (respond to comments)

---

## Phase 3: Content Calendar (Week 3)

Create 30-day posting schedule:
- 5 posts/week
- Mix of pillar types
- Consistent posting times
- Use scheduling tools (Buffer, Later, Meta Business Suite)

---

## Phase 4: Engagement (Week 4)

- Reply to comments within 24 hours
- Follow/engage with competitors' audiences
- Join relevant groups/conversations
- Use relevant hashtags (10-15 per post)

---

## Metrics to Track

- Follower growth/month
- Engagement rate (likes+comments/followers)
- Click-through rate
- Website traffic from social
- Conversions (sales, leads)

---

## Platform Strategy

**Instagram:** Lifestyle + behind-the-scenes + stories
**LinkedIn:** Insights + thought leadership + case studies
**Facebook:** Community building + longer-form content
**TikTok:** Entertaining, authentic, trending sounds
**YouTube:** Educational, evergreen, longer videos

---

## Budget

- Free: Organic posting only
- ₹5,000/month: Social media posting + basic ads
- ₹15,000/month: Full management + content creation + ads
- ₹30,000+/month: Full agency support

---

## Timeline to Results

- Month 1: Consistency established
- Month 2: Engagement rate improves
- Month 3: Follower growth accelerates
- Month 6+: Predictable lead generation

---

## FAQ

**Q: Which platform first?**
A: Where your customers hang out. B2B = LinkedIn. B2C = Instagram.

**Q: How often should I post?**
A: 5/week minimum. Quality over quantity.

**Q: Do I need paid ads?**
A: Organic takes 3-6 months. Ads accelerate to 2-4 weeks.

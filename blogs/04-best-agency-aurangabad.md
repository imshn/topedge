## Red Flags: Avoid These Agencies

❌ **No case studies or results**
- Legit agencies publish results
- Avoid: "We can't share due to NDAs" (translation: no results)

❌ **Cheap pricing (₹5,000-10,000/month)**
- Digital marketing costs ₹20,000+ minimum
- Cheap = low effort = no results

❌ **Only offer one service (just SEO, just ads)**
- Businesses need integrated strategy
- Red flag: "We only do Facebook ads"

❌ **No Google Analytics or reporting**
- Can't measure what they can't track
- Demand access to your GA4 dashboard

❌ **Pressure to sign long contracts**
- Confident agencies offer month-to-month
- Avoid: "Sign 1-year contract or we're done"

---

## Green Flags: Look For These

✅ **Published case studies with results**
- "Increased client sales by 120% in 3 months"
- "Generated ₹50+ leads per month for B2B SaaS"
- Specific numbers = legitimate results

✅ **Clear pricing & deliverables**
- "₹25,000/month includes: SEO, social media, reporting"
- No hidden fees or vague promises

✅ **Multiple service offerings**
- SEO, Google Ads, Meta Ads, content, social media
- Why? Because integrated = better results

✅ **Direct access to your account**
- You can see Google Analytics anytime
- Transparent reporting dashboards
- Monthly performance reports

✅ **Industry expertise**
- They've worked with your type of business
- "We specialize in e-commerce" or "B2B SaaS"
- Case studies in your industry

✅ **Month-to-month contracts**
- Confidence in their work
- No lock-in period needed

## Questions to Ask Any Agency

### Question 1: "What's your process?"
**Good answer:** "Week 1 audit, weeks 2-4 strategy, month 2 launch, months 3+ optimization"
**Bad answer:** "We'll start posting ads and see what happens"

### Question 2: "Can I see case studies in my industry?"
**Good answer:** "Yes, here's a salon client, here's an e-commerce client, here's a service business"
**Bad answer:** "We can't share, NDA" or no case studies

### Question 3: "What's included for ₹[your budget]?"
**Good answer:** Specific list (SEO audit, 4 Google Ads campaigns, social media posting, monthly reporting)
**Bad answer:** "We'll do digital marketing" (too vague)

### Question 4: "How do you measure success?"
**Good answer:** "Leads generated, cost per lead, website traffic increase, conversion rate"
**Bad answer:** "More followers" or "social media engagement"

### Question 5: "What's your average client retention?"
**Good answer:** "80%+ (meaning clients stay, results are good)"
**Bad answer:** "Clients leave after 3 months" = bad results

### Question 6: "Can I access my Google Analytics directly?"
**Good answer:** "Yes, you'll have full access to your GA4 dashboard"
**Bad answer:** "No, we manage that"

## Red Flags Specific to Aurangabad Agencies

⚠️ **"We guarantee #1 Google ranking"**
- No one can guarantee this
- Run away immediately

⚠️ **"Results in 2 weeks"**
- SEO takes 3-6 months
- Ads work fast, but optimization takes time
- Unrealistic promises = scam

⚠️ **"We know someone at Google/Facebook"**
- Irrelevant
- Technical knowledge matters, not connections

⚠️ **Extremely cheap freelancer pricing**
- ₹3,000-5,000/month for "complete digital marketing"
- That's 3-5 hours of work. Can't deliver results.

⚠️ **"We'll build you a website for ₹5,000"**
- Quality = cost
- ₹5,000 websites are filled with bugs, security issues, poor design

## Interview Process: 3-Step Approach

### Step 1: Shortlist (30 minutes)
- Google "digital marketing agency Aurangabad"
- Visit top 5 websites
- Note who has case studies, testimonials, clear pricing

### Step 2: Phone/Video Call (20 minutes)
- Ask the 6 questions above
- Feel out their communication style
- Listen for confidence vs. pitch

### Step 3: Proposal Review (2-3 days)
- Request written proposal with:
  - Current state audit
  - 3-month strategy
  - Monthly deliverables
  - Expected KPIs
  - Pricing breakdown

**Pick the one that best understands YOUR business (not their services).**

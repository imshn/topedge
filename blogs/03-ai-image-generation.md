## Step-by-Step: Generate Your First Product Image

**Step 3:** Type this prompt:
```
/imagine product photography of a luxury watch, gold, white background, studio lighting, 8K, professional product shot
```

**Step 4:** Press Enter and wait (30-60 seconds)
**Step 5:** Choose the best image
**Step 6:** Upscale it to high resolution
**Step 7:** Download and use

## Prompt Engineering: The Secret Sauce

The better your description, the better your AI image.

### Bad Prompt:
"A phone"

### Good Prompt:
"Luxury smartphone, iPhone 15 style, midnight black, front-facing, minimalist white background, professional product photography, studio lighting, depth of field, 8K resolution, trending on Behance"

**Key words that improve results:**
- "Professional photography"
- "Studio lighting" or "Cinematic lighting"
- "8K resolution" or "High quality"
- "White background" or "Minimalist"
- "Award-winning" or "Trending on [platform]"

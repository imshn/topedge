## Part 1: Account Setup (10 Minutes)

### Step 1: Create a Google Ads Account
1. Go to [google.com/ads](https://google.com/ads)
2. Click "Start Now"
3. Sign in with your Google account (or create one)
4. Choose your business country: **India**
5. Add your business website
6. Accept the terms

**Done.** You now have a Google Ads account.

### Step 2: Link Google Ads to Google Analytics 4
This lets you track which ads actually drive sales (crucial!).

1. Go to Tools & Settings → Linked Accounts
2. Select Google Analytics
3. Choose your GA4 property
4. Click "Link"

**Why:** Without this, you're flying blind. You won't know if your ads make money.

### Step 3: Set Up Billing
1. Click your profile icon (top right) → Billing
2. Add your credit/debit card
3. Set a monthly budget (e.g., ₹20,000)
4. Click "Save"

**Pro Tip:** Start with ₹20,000/month. Scale up only after 2 weeks of profitable data.

## Part 2: Create Your First Campaign (20 Minutes)

### Step 4: Choose Your Campaign Goal

Google offers options. Pick ONE:

| Goal | Use Case | Best For |
|------|----------|----------|
| **Sales** | People buying online | E-commerce |
| **Leads** | People filling forms | Agencies, consulting |
| **Website Traffic** | People visiting your site | Blog, content |
| **App Promotion** | People downloading apps | Mobile apps |
| **Local Services** | People calling/visiting | Salons, restaurants |

**For Aurangabad agencies:** Choose **"Leads"** (people requesting consultations).

### Step 5: Set Up Campaign Settings

1. **Campaign Name:** "Aurangabad Digital Marketing - July 2026"
2. **Campaign Type:** Search (Google Search Network)
3. **Subtype:** Standard (not Smart Bidding yet)
4. **Networks:** Google Search + Google Search Partners (check both)
5. **Locations:** Aurangabad, Chh. Sambhajinagar, Mumbai, India
6. **Languages:** English, Hindi
7. **Budget:** ₹1,000/day (₹30,000/month)
8. **Bid Strategy:** Click "Maximize Conversions" (let Google optimize for you)

**Save and continue.**

### Step 6: Create Your First Ad Group

**What's an ad group?** A bundle of related keywords + ads. You can have multiple ad groups per campaign.

**Example Ad Group: "Digital Marketing Agency Services"**

1. Name: "Digital Marketing Agency"
2. Keywords (add these):
   - digital marketing agency aurangabad
   - best digital marketing agency chh sambhajinagar
   - performance marketing services india
   - seo agency aurangabad
   - social media marketing company

**How to add keywords:**
- Type each keyword
- Press Enter
- Continue until done

**Bid Amount:** Set to ₹100/click (start here, adjust later based on performance)

## Part 4: Set Landing Pages (5 Minutes)

### Step 8: Optimize Your Landing Pages

Your ad clicks someone → they land on your page. If your page doesn't convert, you waste money.

**Best practice:**
- Ad about "SEO Services" → lands on SEO service page (not homepage)
- Ad about "Packages" → lands on packages page
- Ad about "Contact us" → lands on contact page

**For beginners:** At minimum, ensure:
- ✓ Page matches ad headline
- ✓ Clear call-to-action button (Get Quote, Call Now, Book Consultation)
- ✓ Mobile-friendly (60% of clicks are mobile)
- ✓ Fast loading (under 3 seconds)

**Everything looks good?** Click "Publish Campaign."

### Step 10: Monitor Daily (First 2 Weeks)

Your campaign is live. Now watch it like a hawk for 2 weeks.

**Check daily:**
- Impressions: How many people saw your ad?
- Clicks: How many clicked it?
- Cost per click: Are you paying too much?
- Conversions: Did they actually contact you or buy?
- Conversion rate: % of clicks that converted

**Dashboard view:**
1. Click your campaign name
2. You'll see: Impressions | Clicks | Cost | Conversions

**Warning signs (pause and fix):**
- ❌ Low click rate (under 1%) = Bad keywords or ad copy
- ❌ High cost per click (over ₹200) = Compete less or improve quality score
- ❌ No conversions after 100 clicks = Landing page problem

### Step 11: Optimize (After 2 Weeks)

Once you have data, improve:

**If high clicks, low conversions:**
- Improve landing page (clearer CTA, testimonials, trust signals)
- Add phone number prominently
- Simplify form (ask only email/phone, not 10 questions)

**If low clicks:**
- Improve ad headlines (make them more compelling)
- Add call extensions (Google shows your phone number automatically)
- Add location extensions (shows your Aurangabad address)

**If high cost per click:**
- Improve Quality Score (better keywords + relevant ads + good landing page)
- Pause underperforming keywords
- Adjust bids down on expensive keywords

## Common Beginner Mistakes (Avoid These)

❌ **Mistake #1:** Targeting all of India
- You run out of budget fast on irrelevant clicks
- **Fix:** Target Aurangabad + Chh. Sambhajinagar only

❌ **Mistake #2:** Generic ads ("We're the best!")
- No one clicks
- **Fix:** Be specific ("Google Ads | Meta Ads | SEO Services in Aurangabad")

❌ **Mistake #3:** Sending all traffic to homepage
- People don't know where to go
- **Fix:** Send "SEO" ad clickers to /service.html, "Packages" clicks to /packages.html

❌ **Mistake #4:** Not using Google Analytics
- You don't know if ads make money
- **Fix:** Link GA4 immediately (Step 2 above)

❌ **Mistake #5:** Giving up after 1 week
- Google Ads needs 2-4 weeks to optimize
- **Fix:** Budget for at least ₹50,000 before judging performance

# Lead Generation Funnel: How Top Agencies Qualify Leads

**Published:** July 2026 | **Reading Time:** 8 min | **By:** Top Edge MediaWorks

## The Funnel

**AWARENESS** → **INTEREST** → **CONSIDERATION** → **DECISION** → **CUSTOMER**

---

## Stage 1: Awareness (Top of Funnel)

Get discovered via:
- Google Ads (high search volume keywords)
- Social media ads (broad audience)
- Content marketing (blog posts, videos)
- SEO (organic search)

**Goal:** 1,000+ impressions

---

## Stage 2: Interest (Consideration)

Capture attention via:
- Lead magnets (free guide, audit, consultation)
- Email signup
- Retargeting ads
- Content upgrades

**Goal:** 100+ email subscribers

---

## Stage 3: Consideration (Decision)

Nurture prospects via:
- Email sequences (5-7 emails over 2 weeks)
- Case studies
- Testimonials
- Pricing transparency

**Goal:** 10-20 "hot" leads ready to talk

---

## Stage 4: Decision (Close)

Convert via:
- Sales call
- Proposal
- Risk-free trial
- Money-back guarantee

**Goal:** 2-5 customers from 100 leads = 2-5% conversion

---

## Real Funnel Example: B2B SaaS

**Impressions:** 10,000 (ads shown)
**Clicks:** 500 (2-5% CTR)
**Email signups:** 50 (10% conversion)
**Hot leads:** 5 (10% of emails)
**Customers:** 1 (20% close rate)

**Cost:**
- Ad spend: ₹50,000
- Cost per customer: ₹50,000
- If LTV = ₹5,00,000 → ROI = 900%

---

## Key Metrics

- Impression-to-Click rate (CTR): Aim 2-5%
- Click-to-Signup rate: Aim 10-20%
- Email-to-Lead rate: Aim 10-15%
- Lead-to-Customer rate: Aim 10-30% (B2B), 1-5% (B2C)

---

## How to Improve Each Stage

**Low CTR?** Improve ad copy, landing page relevance
**Low signups?** Improve lead magnet value
**Low hot leads?** Improve email sequence
**Low customers?** Improve sales process, pricing, guarantees

---

## FAQ

**Q: What's a good conversion rate?**
A: Depends on stage. Click-to-email: 10-20%. Email-to-customer: 5-20%.

**Q: How long is the sales cycle?**
A: B2B: 4-12 weeks. B2C: 1-4 weeks.

**Q: Should I use automations?**
A: Yes. Email automation saves time, increases consistency.

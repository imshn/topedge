# Website vs Mobile App: Which Should Your Business Build First?

**Published:** July 2026 | **Reading Time:** 7 min | **By:** Top Edge MediaWorks

## The Question: Website or App?

**Short answer:** Website first. App later (if at all).

---

## Comparison

| Factor | Website | App |
|--------|---------|-----|
| Cost | ₹20k-2L | ₹2L-20L |
| Time | 2-8 weeks | 3-6 months |
| Maintenance | Easy | Hard |
| SEO | Yes | No |
| Users | Everyone | Users who download |
| ROI | High | Medium |

---

## Website: The Safe Bet

**Advantages:**
- Reach 100% of internet users
- SEO brings free organic traffic
- Lower cost, faster launch
- No app store approval needed
- Easier to update

**When to start:** Immediately

---

## Mobile App: The Long Game

**Advantages:**
- Direct access to users (push notifications)
- App can work offline
- Feels premium/professional
- Sticky (users keep it installed)

**When to start:** After website proves market demand

---

## Real Case Study: E-commerce Startup

**Phase 1: Website Launch**
- Cost: ₹50,000
- Time: 4 weeks
- Month 1-3 revenue: ₹5,00,000
- Users: 100+ monthly

**Phase 2: Mobile App Launch (after 6 months)**
- Cost: ₹15,00,000
- Time: 4 months
- App revenue: Additional ₹3,00,000/month (increased repeat purchases)

**Lesson:** Website generated ₹20M+ before spending on app. App made sense because market validated.

---

## When to Build an App

✅ You have 10,000+ active website users
✅ Users ask for an app
✅ Your business benefits from offline access
✅ You can afford ongoing development costs

❌ You don't have product-market fit yet
❌ Your website isn't profitable
❌ App is "nice to have" not "must have"

---

## The Hybrid Approach (Best)

1. **Month 0-3:** Build website, validate market
2. **Month 3-6:** Grow website traffic to 1,000+ monthly users
3. **Month 6-12:** Decide if app is worth the cost
4. **Month 12+:** Build app if justified by data

---

## FAQ

**Q: Can I skip the website and just build an app?**
A: Not recommended. Website reaches everyone. App requires users to download.

**Q: Is web app (like PWA) enough?**
A: For most businesses, yes. Web apps handle 90% of use cases.

**Q: What if I only have ₹2L budget?**
A: Build a great website. Wait on app until revenue justifies it.

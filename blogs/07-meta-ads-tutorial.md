# Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram) Tutorial: How to Reach Local Customers in Aurangabad

**Published:** July 2026 | **Reading Time:** 9 min | **By:** Top Edge MediaWorks

## Why Meta Ads Are Perfect for Local Businesses

Facebook + Instagram reach 2.5 billion people daily. For local businesses in Aurangabad? That's 50,000+ local business owners in your area.

---

## What Are Meta Ads?

Ads on Facebook and Instagram. Target people by interests, location, behavior. Cost: ₹20-100 per click.

---

## Setup (10 minutes)

1. Go to meta.com/business
2. Create Business Account
3. Link Facebook/Instagram pages
4. Add payment method
5. Click "Create Campaign"

---

## Campaign Setup

**Goal:** Choose "Leads" (for consulting businesses)

**Audience:**
- Location: Aurangabad + 50km radius
- Age: 25-55
- Interests: "Small business", "Digital marketing", "Entrepreneurship"

**Budget:** ₹1,000/day (₹30,000/month)

**Ad Type:** Carousel (showing multiple services)

---

## Example Ads for Aurangabad Agency

**Ad 1: Digital Marketing Services**
- Image: Professional team photo
- Headline: "Digital Marketing Agency Aurangabad"
- Text: "SEO, Google Ads, Social Media. Free consultation."
- CTA: "Learn More"

**Ad 2: Case Studies**
- Video: 30-second case study highlight
- Headline: "See Our Results"
- Text: "45 businesses grew with us in 2026"
- CTA: "View Case Studies"

## Optimization (After 2 Weeks)

- Low click rate? Improve creative/copy
- High clicks, no conversions? Improve landing page
- High cost? Narrow audience or lower bid

## FAQ

**Q: What's the difference between lead ads and link clicks?**
A: Lead ads capture emails directly. Link clicks send to website. Lead ads = faster collection.

**Q: Can I target just Aurangabad?**
A: Yes. Use "Aurangabad" + 10-30km radius for best results.

**Q: How long before I see leads?**
A: 3-5 days for first clicks, 1-2 weeks for first conversions.
